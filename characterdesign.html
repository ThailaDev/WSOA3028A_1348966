Web Design Rationale

I want the website to show Ali as a skateboarder and as a South African Queer woman.

To achieve this I will be posting in Ali's Thoughts about how she, as a character, feels about being a woman in skateboarding.

I do not want the colour schemes to be traditionally girly but also not too masculine so I have decided on a nutral colour pallet.

I want the website to be easy to navigate and minimalist as to not overwhelm viewers.

Web Design Rationale 2.0

I would still love to go with the ideas in my previous wireframe however with lockdown and 2020 just being the worst overall I will probably have to find ways to simplify it.

I have been struggling with motivation issues and mental health during this time so yes, simplifying my designs will be better for me but I hope to stick to as much of the design as humanly possible.

Ali's Character

Ali is a young female South African Skateboarder who stands up for women and LGBTQIA+ rights because she is both a female and part of the LGBTQIA+ community.

I made her a skateboarder because I am interested in skateboarding and it is easier to write about things when you, yourself are interested in them.

Ali is loosely based on myself as I do skate casually and I am also a woman who is part of the LGBTQIA+ community.

I, Thaila Delport, am very passionate about woman and LGBTQIA+ rights so I thouht it would be appropriate to make ALi like that as well.
