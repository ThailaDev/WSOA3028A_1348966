About Ali

About Me

My name is Ali Michaels and I am a 24 year old skater from Johannesburg.

Skating and Proffession

I have been skating almost every day since I was 15 years old. I have been working at Johannesburg's best skate park for over two years now, situated in Bryanston. My duties at work include managing park memberships, taking daily payments from customers, organising and taking stock of the skate shop and keeping the park neat and tidy.

In the beginning of 2020 Johannesburg set up its own skate club. Being part of a club is imperiitive in the process of being a part of the Olympics.

Accomplishments

I have won 4 small competitions held at YBF skatepark in 2012, 2013, 2017 and 2018.

In 2019 I competed in the Kimberly Diamond Cup in the women's section and came 1st overall.

The 2020 Olympics

I am thankful to have the opportunity to compete in the Olympic qualifiers for 2020. Hopefully soon I will be able to compete with some of the finest skaters in the world in Tokyo.
